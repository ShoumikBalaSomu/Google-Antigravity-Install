Deploy Google Antigravity Instantly

Deploy, update, or cleanly reinstall the Google Antigravity developer suite on Fedora. Zero manual download required.

bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ShoumikBalaSomu/Google-Antigravity-Install/main/reinstall_antigravity.sh)"

This command downloads, extracts, and sets up launcher shortcuts for CLI, Desktop app, and IDE.
